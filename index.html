These are typographic,
original and exclusive
creations whose singularity
reflects our requirements
and our taste.
Some, originally conceived
as part of a graphic design
commission or the result
or personal research,
are just waiting for you
to take off to new horizons.
Others have the ambition
to enter your daily life
as partners you can always
count on, effective
typographic tools
and without qualms.
Finally, if graphic design is a social practice,
why not take advantage of the multiple opportunities
it offers us to collaborate with talented personalities ?
Some of these creations will reflect these fortuitous
or provoked occasions.
